OUR PROGRAM

Our 9-month program is divided into 12 modules. The last module is a 6-week intensive Final Project where students work on a real project with a real partner. Our training takes place at Epitech (Brussels) every Sunday. During the week, students are supported online in their self-study, homework, and peer learning.

Our Core Values

FAQ

What is HackYourFuture Belgium?

HackYourFuture Belgium is a non-profit organization that provides free web development training to refugees and people from underprivileged backgrounds. Our mission is to empower individuals and create a diverse and inclusive tech industry.

How can I apply?

You can apply online by filling out the application form on our website. Please make sure to read the eligibility criteria before applying. Our team will review your application and get back to you within two weeks.

Is the program really free?

Yes, the program is completely free of charge. We cover all expenses related to the program, including laptops, textbooks, and internet access. Our goal is to make education accessible to everyone.

What kind of support do you offer after graduation?

We offer career support and guidance after graduation. Our team works closely with our graduates to help them find employment opportunities in the tech industry. We also provide mentorship and networking opportunities to our alumni.
